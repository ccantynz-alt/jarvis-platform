# Accounting knowledge pack — Singapore (seed, verify everything against iras.gov.sg)
- GST: 9% (raised in steps recently — verify current). Registration threshold S$1M taxable turnover (verify). Returns usually quarterly, due 1 month after period.
- Corporate income tax: 17% flat with partial exemptions for smaller companies; startup exemption scheme may apply (verify eligibility).
- Filing rhythm: ECI within 3 months of financial year end; Form C-S/C by 30 Nov.
- ACRA: annual return within 7 months of FYE for private companies (verify).
- Payroll: CPF contributions for citizens/PRs by 14th of following month; IR8A for employees annually.
- Records: keep 5 years.
- Authorities: IRAS + ACRA.
