# Accounting knowledge pack — United States (seed; federal + STATE layers, verify against irs.gov and state authority)
- No national VAT/GST. Sales tax is per-state, with economic nexus rules for online sales (thresholds ~$100k or 200 transactions per state, varies — verify per state where customers are).
- Federal income tax: C-corp flat 21% (verify); pass-throughs report on owners' returns. Estimated taxes quarterly (Apr/Jun/Sep/Jan 15 rhythm — verify).
- Payroll: federal withholding + FICA; Forms 941 quarterly, W-2/1099-NEC in January.
- Entity state matters (e.g. Delaware franchise tax, annual reports per state of registration/foreign qualification).
- Records: keep at least 3-7 years depending on item.
- Authorities: IRS + each relevant state department of revenue. US complexity is high — licensed CPA involvement is strongly recommended before any filing.
