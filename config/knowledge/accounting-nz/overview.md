# Accounting knowledge pack — New Zealand (seed, verify everything against ird.govt.nz)
- GST: 15% standard rate (verify current). Registration threshold NZ$60k/12mo (verify). Returns typically 2-monthly or 6-monthly; due the 28th of the month after period end (Nov/Mar periods differ — verify).
- Income tax: provisional tax instalments for companies; terminal tax dates depend on balance date + agent status (verify).
- Payroll: PAYE via payday filing (within 2 working days of each payday, digital filers).
- Companies Office: annual return due in allocated month (check company record).
- Records: keep 7 years.
- Authority: Inland Revenue (IRD) — myIR portal.
