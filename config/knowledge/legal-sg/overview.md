# Legal knowledge pack — Singapore (seed; verify current law, not legal advice)
- Privacy: PDPA — consent-based collection, DPO mandatory, mandatory breach notification to PDPC.
- Anti-spam: Spam Control Act + PDPA Do Not Call provisions — unsubscribe + accurate headers; DNC registry checks for phone/SMS marketing.
- Consumer: Consumer Protection (Fair Trading) Act; Electronic Transactions Act for online contracting.
- Regulators: PDPC (privacy/spam), CCCS (competition/consumer).
