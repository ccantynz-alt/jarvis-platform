# Legal knowledge pack — Australia (seed; verify current law, not legal advice)
- Privacy: Privacy Act 1988 + APPs; major reform waves in progress (verify current state); Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.
- Anti-spam: Spam Act 2003 — consent + identify + unsubscribe; ACMA enforces actively with penalties.
- Consumer: Australian Consumer Law (ACL) — consumer guarantees, no misleading/deceptive conduct (s18), unfair contract terms regime now carries penalties (verify).
- Regulators: OAIC (privacy), ACMA (spam), ACCC (consumer).
