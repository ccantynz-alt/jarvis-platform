# Legal knowledge pack — United States (seed; federal + STATE patchwork, verify current law, not legal advice)
- Privacy: no single federal law — state patchwork led by California (CCPA/CPRA), plus VA/CO/CT/UT and a growing list (verify which states apply by customer base). COPPA if any under-13 users.
- Anti-spam: CAN-SPAM — opt-OUT model (unlike NZ/AU/UK): no prior consent needed but sender ID, physical address, honoring unsubscribe within 10 days are mandatory. State laws can add more.
- Consumer: FTC Act §5 (unfair/deceptive practices), state UDAP laws; auto-renewal/subscription disclosure laws in several states (verify).
- Regulators: FTC, state attorneys general.
