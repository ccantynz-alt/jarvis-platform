# Accounting knowledge pack — Australia (seed, verify everything against ato.gov.au)
- GST: 10% (verify current). Registration threshold A$75k turnover (verify).
- BAS (Business Activity Statement): usually quarterly, due ~28 days after quarter end (Q2 due 28 Feb) — verify exact dates yearly.
- Income tax: company rate 25% for base-rate entities / 30% otherwise (verify).
- Payroll: PAYG withholding; Single Touch Payroll (STP) reporting each pay event; superannuation guarantee quarterly (rate stepping — verify current %).
- ASIC: annual review fee on registration anniversary.
- Records: keep 5 years.
- Authority: Australian Taxation Office (ATO) + ASIC.
