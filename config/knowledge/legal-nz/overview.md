# Legal knowledge pack — New Zealand (seed; verify current law, not legal advice)
- Privacy: Privacy Act 2020 — 13 IPPs, mandatory notifiable privacy breach reporting to OPC, privacy officer required.
- Anti-spam: Unsolicited Electronic Messages Act 2007 — CONSENT REQUIRED (express/inferred/deemed), sender ID + unsubscribe mandatory. Directly governs any cold-email funnel from NZ.
- Consumer: Consumer Guarantees Act + Fair Trading Act (no misleading conduct; applies to online claims/pricing).
- Contracts: standard NZ law; Electronic Transactions coverage under Contract and Commercial Law Act 2017.
- Regulators: Office of the Privacy Commissioner, Commerce Commission, DIA (spam).
